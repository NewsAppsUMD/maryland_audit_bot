School Audits Overview
(cid:190) Audits are performed in accordance with State law,
which requires OLA to audit all 24 public school
systems once over a six year period. Audit reports
have been issued for 18 school systems to date.
(cid:190) The purpose of the audits is to evaluate the
effectiveness and efficiency of a school system’s
financial management practices in safeguarding
assets and its use of financial resources.
(cid:190) Audit approach consists of 11 functional areas
previously approved by the Joint Audit Committee,
as required by State law.
